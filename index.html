About me

I am a molecular biologist focused on experimental research, with experience in cell cultivation, reagent preparation, host–pathogen interactions, and ecological modeling. My training emphasizes clear laboratory practice, including aseptic technique and GMP-aligned workflows that ensure accuracy, reproducibility, and compliance. I have experience preparing media, maintaining calibrated instrumentation, and designing experiments that address complex biological questions. Alongside this, I bring complementary skills in coding and bioinformatics, which allow me to build pipelines and data-driven tools that enhance analysis and streamline lab workflows. My ultimate goal is to advance molecular biology research through careful experimentation, while leveraging computational approaches when they can extend the impact of my work.
