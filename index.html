Marker Finder

Primer design wizard with QC badges, conservation plots, and NCBI-powered search. CSV/PDF export.

Open app

Designing reliable primers is one of the most critical yet time-consuming steps in molecular biology. A poorly chosen primer set can waste days of work, valuable reagents, and entire experiments.

Marker Finder streamlines this process by integrating primer design, conservation analysis, and NCBI-powered validation into a single tool. By giving users immediate quality checks and exportable results, it reduces the risk of experimental failure and helps researchers move from concept to data generation more efficiently.

In both academic and applied labs, time saved at the design stage often translates directly into faster discoveries and reduced costs, making this tool a practical bridge between concept and result.

Lab Inventory

Inventory management for research labs: items, attachments, reorder queue, analytics.

Open app

Every lab depends on careful reagent management to ensure experiments are reproducible and compliant with quality standards. Mismanaged inventories can lead to expired reagents, interrupted workflows, and even compliance risks in regulated environments.

The Lab Inventory app models real-world laboratory logistics by tracking items, usage, and reorders in a structured system. For a new graduate or early-career researcher, building such a tool demonstrates not only technical skills but also an understanding of the daily operational challenges scientists face.

In practice, this type of system ensures experiments aren’t derailed by missing supplies and helps labs align with GMP expectations.

About Me

My foundation in both organismal and molecular sciences allows me to bridge experimental research with systems-level insight. I specialize in cell cultivation, reagent preparation, and host–pathogen biology, supported by rigorous training in GMP-aligned workflows and experimental design.
